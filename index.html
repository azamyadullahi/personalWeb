Transfer of commodities or people from one place to another place is known as transport the things through which transfer takes place are known as vehicles or motors. Bullock-carts, rickshaws, cars, buses, trucks, trains, boats, ships, aero planes and helicopters etc. are examples of different vehicles. All these vehicles need to ply on different ways or routes. Cars, buses, bullock carts and rickshaws ply on the roads.
